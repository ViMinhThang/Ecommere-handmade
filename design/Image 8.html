Master Weaver

Elena Vance

Based in the rugged highlands of the Isle of Skye, Elena specializes in hand-dyed organic wool and linen. Her work is a dialogue between ancient weaving techniques and contemporary minimalist aesthetics.

About the Maker

Elena’s journey began in a small coastal village, watching the rhythmic motion of her grandmother’s pedal loom. Today, she maintains that heritage, sourcing wool from local crofters and dyeing each batch by hand using gathered lichens, gorse, and heather.

"The process is slow," Elena explains. "It requires a deep patience that modern production has forgotten. A single throw can take up to forty hours of focused labor. But that weight—the physical presence of the hand in the fabric—is what makes it home."
