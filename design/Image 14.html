Made with Intent

Each Vesica vase undergoes a rigorous 14-day process. Starting as a raw 12-pound block of clay, it is wheel-thrown to find its initial form, then aged in a humidity-controlled chamber for 48 hours.

The "chattering" texture on the surface is achieved by carving into the leather-hard clay with hand-forged steel tools, a technique that requires years of muscle memory to master.
